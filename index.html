About

Cycling is the most efficient form of transport and all cyclists have the right to use the road just like any other vehicle.

This means that cyclists must learn the road rules and signals to keep themselves and other road users safe from unwanted incidents.

At Nigeria Cycling Embassy, we are committed to creating a strong cycling community in Nigeria and empowering cyclists in Nigeria with important information to be safe and efficient
